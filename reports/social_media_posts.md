## LinkedIn

I have completed a new Power BI portfolio project: **Hamburg Public Expenditure Dashboard**.

Using official Hamburg expenditure data for 2010-2012, I transformed a complex statistical source table into a three-page report covering executive KPIs, expenditure trends, variance analysis, and interactive category detail.

The project helped me strengthen my practical skills in Power Query, DAX, data modeling, financial reporting logic, and dashboard design. I also documented important limitations in the accounting hierarchy so the results are communicated responsibly.
